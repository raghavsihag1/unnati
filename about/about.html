Who We Are

Unnati: The Knowledge Hub For Civil Aspirants is a not-for-profit organisation registered under the government of India. We aim to provide free guidance to talented UPSC aspirants who lack adequate resources.

Founded on December 18, 2016, Unnati connects students with Members of Parliament, Civil Services officers, and professors to guide them toward a better path. We believe that theoretical knowledge alone is not enough, which is why we organize events, seminars, and internship opportunities to provide holistic exposure.

Our Journey

"Alone we can do so little, together we can do so much."

Unnati was started by Mr. Lokesh Chugh under the able guidance of Mr. Oscar Fernandes (MP, Rajya Sabha and Chief Patron, Unnati). Over the years, we have grown from a small initiative into a family that stands together through thick and thin. We celebrate our foundation day every December 18th, marking years of molding the youth into responsible citizens and assets for the nation.

Our Achievements

International Recognition

Received a Letter of Appreciation from the Republic of Lebanon. His Excellency Mr. Rabie Narsh (Ambassador) honored Unnati for our service to society.

Conquering Heights

Team member Nitish Singh successfully conquered Mt. Stok Kangri and hoisted Unnati's flag at the peak, symbolizing our audacity and courage.

Civil Services Seminars

Conducted interactive sessions at Hansraj College with experienced officers like Jatin Narval, Dr. Sudhir, and Amitabh Khare to guide aspirants.

Model United Nations

Organized MUNs in various schools (e.g., Evergreen Public School) to inculcate debate culture, with over 450 delegates discussing sustainable development.
