Unnati on Campus

Internship Program for College Students

About the Program

Unnati on Campus is our flagship internship program designed to provide college students with practical exposure to NGO operations, work culture learning, and skill development opportunities. It is perfect for students who want to make a difference while gaining valuable professional experience.

Program Benefits

Practical NGO exposure
Certificate of completion
Work culture learning
Networking with aspirants
Leadership skills
Professional mentorship

Opportunities Available

Content Writing

Create impactful content for our publications and social media

Event Management

Organize and coordinate impactful events and programs

Social Media

Manage our digital presence and engage with the community

Who Should Apply?

  • College students from any discipline
  • Passionate about social work and youth empowerment
  • Looking for practical work experience
  • Committed to making a positive impact
