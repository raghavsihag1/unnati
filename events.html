Events & Initiatives

We believe that theoretical knowledge alone is insufficient. To provide holistic exposure, we organize parliamentary visits, marathons, quizzes, and interactive sessions with civil servants.

Mahapanchayat MUN

Date: August 27-28, 2019  •  Venue: Evergreen Public School

Unnati organized the first edition of the Mahapanchayat MUN, a national-level conference hosting over 500 delegates. The event focused on inculcating a culture of debate and discussion among youth. His Excellency Rabie Narsh (Ambassador of Lebanon) graced the event to award the winners.

Parliamentary Visit

Participants: 1500+ Students

We hosted a visit to the Parliament of India for our interns to understand the working of the government firsthand. The essence of democratic debate became clearer when students witnessed the institution itself.

Run For Education Excellence

Guest: Mr. Harsh Beniwal

A 6km marathon organized to promote education and generate proceeds for charity. Over 2000 students participated, helping us adopt a slum community now known as Udyat.

Civil Services Interaction

Venue: Hansraj College

An interactive session for 1700+ aspirants with honored officers like Jatin Narval and Dr. Sudhir. Students had the rare opportunity to ask questions directly to their role models.

Erudite: The Quiz

Guests: Amit Bhadana & Harsh Deep Ahuja

A competitive quiz conducted by IPS officers Mr. Sudhir and Mr. Amit. The event focused on major topics for civil service exams, ensuring a knowledge-enriching time for all 2500+ participants.

Samvidhan se Vidhan tak

Duration: 3-Day Session

A seminar dedicated to reading and explaining the Constitution of India. The goal was to eradicate unawareness about citizens' rights and the judicial measures available to them.

Parliamentary Debate

Chief Guest: Sharmishtha Mukherjee Ji

Students from all over India participated in this massive debate. Judges were moved by the research and enthusiasm of young children discussing complex national issues.

Indo-Korean Meet

Focus: International Relations

Delegates from Korea joined Unnati interns to discuss issues of international importance. The cultural exchange aimed to improve relations and expand the horizons of our students' knowledge.

Self Analysis Test

Date: Feb 10, 2019

A workshop on "How to crack Civil Service Examination" followed by a short self-analysis test for 300 aspirants to gauge their preparedness.
