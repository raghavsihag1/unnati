About — Mission & History

Unnati is registered as a not-for-profit organisation focused on educating and empowering youth. It began in December 2016 and runs programs to widen access to education and test-prep resources.

Our Branches

  • Uthan — A youth parliament that simulates parliamentary debates and policy discussion.
  • Unnati Times — A youth-led monthly newspaper covering news, quizzes, interviews and features.
  • Udyat — Outreach program focused on teaching and mentoring children in under-served communities.

Events & Programs

We host workshops, mock interviews, youth parliaments and community outreach programs. Events are announced on this page with registration details and schedules.
